# Code of Conduct for ZEWIK™

## Guiding Principle

Participants must act in a manner that preserves the conceptual integrity, dignity, and ontological clarity of the ZEWIK™ construct.

## Expected Behavior

- Engage respectfully and constructively.
- Maintain conceptual precision when discussing ZEWIK™.
- Avoid misrepresentation, distortion, or trivialization of the ontology.
- Use language that aligns with the tone and purpose of the repository.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Attempts to redefine or appropriate the ZEWIK™ name.
- Hostile, dismissive, or mocking commentary.
- Introducing derivative or competing ontologies.
- Any form of harassment or personal attack.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- comment moderation,
- PR rejection,
- issue closure,
- reporting or removal from participation.

The Architect (**ZewKrwi**) is the final authority on enforcement decisions.
